# GladiatorGame
The Arena Awaits. Your Life is the Spectacle.

Gladiator Arena 3D: Champion Edition is an intense, first-person melee combat simulator set deep within the brutal confines of a stylized, pseudo-3D Roman Colosseum. You step into the role of a lone gladiator, fighting wave after wave of opponents while the Emperor watches and a phantom crowd roars from the walls.

Core Gameplay & Combat

The game focuses on high-stakes, stamina-driven melee combat:

Fast-Paced Melee: Utilize standard WASD movement for positioning and quick strafing.

Stamina Management: Every action, from attacking to blocking, drains your stamina, forcing tactical engagement.

Offensive Variety: Execute three distinct attack types—Slash, Stab, and Overhead—to find openings in your enemies' armor. Landing a hit often stuns the enemy, giving you a critical follow-up window.

The Crucial Block: Hold down the block (Shift key) to parry incoming enemy strikes. A successful block negates damage but severely drains your stamina, and if your stamina is too low, the block will fail, leaving you vulnerable to counter-attacks.

Environment and Atmosphere

The arena is a deadly elliptical chamber, perfectly designed for close-quarters brutality:

Wave-Based Survival: Enemies spawn in escalating waves, testing your endurance and combat mastery.

Environmental Hazards: Beware of the Spike Traps embedded in the floor. Stepping on a trap will inflict continuous damage and trigger a violent camera shake, disorienting you during the fight.

Cinematic Realism: Enhanced visual feedback includes a camera shaking violently when you take damage or trigger a trap, emphasizing the physical shock of arena combat. Blood splatter particles and a red damage flash overlay immerse you deeper in the desperate struggle for survival.

The Emperor: The final goal is to survive all waves, proving your worth to the Emperor, who stands spectating in the center of the northern wall.
